Virtual Invigilation System using Computer Vision Techniques

A virtual invigilation system using computer vision is a technology-driven approach to monitoring and supervising online exams or assessments. It typically involves the use of cameras and specialized software to track and analyze test-takers' behavior and activities during the exam to ensure academic integrity.

While virtual invigilation systems can help deter cheating and maintain exam integrity in online settings, it's essential to balance security measures with considerations for test-taker privacy and accessibility. Additionally, effective implementation requires clear communication with students about the purpose and use of the system to promote trust and compliance.
